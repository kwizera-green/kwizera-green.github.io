About Us

Jeremie Green Climate Association is a youth-led initiative from Rwanda, fighting climate change through tree planting and environmental education in Nyagatare, Gatsibo, and Gicumbi.

Projects

  • Tree Planting
  • Tree Nurseries
  • Green Leader Training
  • Awareness Campaigns
